Sigma

Requirements

To unlock Sigma you need to complete a special project. This project starts with a mean time of 20 years after you research Alpha. The project takes 5 years to complete but can fail during these 5 years, forcing you to restart it. After completing the special project you will gain a research that requires a scientist hat is at least level 10 and has one of the following traits:

  • Erudite
  • Spark of Genius
  • Chosen One
  • Brainslug
  • Curator
  • Sapient AI Assistant
  • Materials Expertise

Strategy for obtaining

Sigma is the tier of technology that has the fewest requirements. Except for a good scientist you can dedicate your resources on preparing for Phi.
