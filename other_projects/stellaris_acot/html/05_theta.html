Theta

Requirements

Unlocking Theta is rather simple, it consists of excavating two digsites and researching a few technologies before building the Aetherophasic Mirror and summoning a Shroud Master.

Strategy for obtaining

The two digsites will spawn in the systems 'Celeste' and 'The Golden Archives'. So, if there is ever an opportunity throughout the game for you to get these systems, do it. It will speed up the process significantly.
