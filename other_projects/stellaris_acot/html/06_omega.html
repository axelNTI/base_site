Omega

Requirements

Omega is by far the hardest tier of technology to unlock. It consists of opening an outer gate and then fighting the Stellarborne which are using Omega. You will get the ability to unlock the outer gates as soon as the mid-game starts.

Strategy for obtaining

The only thing that matters for Omega is your Fleet. The Stellarborne is incredibly powerful and will wipe out basically anything. The only technology that can even compare to Omega is Theta. Use those weapons on the best ship ACOT has to offer, the Herculean. If you ever think you are ready to face the Stellarborne, you are probably not. You can take all time you need. When you have Theta, nothing except the Stellarborne can challenge your power. Only open the outer gates when you have a ridiculous amount of Herculeans.
