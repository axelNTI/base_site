Alpha

Requirements

To obtain Alpha you need to obtain the Precursor Databank which will unlock the researches necessary to unlock Alpha. The databank has a few different ways to obtain it. Building it on your own, getting it as a favor from the fallen empires, winning a war against a fallen empire, and buying it from the Curators.

Strategy for obtaining

Although there are plethora of ways to obtain the databank, one stands out as the quickest. Buying it from the Curators, to do that you need a large quantity of resources. Namely 10 000 Dark Matter, 10 000 Dark Energy, and 100 000 Energy Credits. Alongside this, you still need a large quantity of research to actually research Alpha, so do not forget to continue improving your research.
