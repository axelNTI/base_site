Phi

Requirements

Unlocking Phi is one of the harder challenges in the mod. You will face an enemy with far superior technology, namely Phi itself. To face said enemy you need to build a Dimensional Portal. The event that allows you to research the portal will have a mean time to happen of 20 years after a few conditions are met. The countdown will start when you have researched the most advanced Stellarite reactor and done the reimagination project for the precursor starbase. All reimagination projects can be found in the precursor database. After building the dimensional portal, activate it. You will need to defeat the fleet that spawns, and then the invade the vault. This process will repeat a few times before you unlock Phi.

Strategy for obtaining

Unlocking Phi can be broken down into two aspects. Starting the countdown and fighting. Doing the reimagination project is extremely expensive, both in terms of resources but also research. As soon as you have unlocked Alpha, you should start focusing on building up your economy and research while unlocking Sigma. As soon as the requirements are met and the 20 year countdown has started you should switch your focus to your military. You will fight both in space and on the ground so do not forget your ground forces. You will need a large fleet with top of the line Sigma weapons with a good general to even stand a chance. If you barely win one of the fights, take a break and build up more since the next one will be harder.
