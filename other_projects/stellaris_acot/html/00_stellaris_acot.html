Summary

Ancient Cache of Technologies or ACOT for short is one of the most popular and expansive mods for Stellaris. This website will cover not only the most the mod itself but also its most popular submods such as Secrets Beyond The Gates which adds Omega, Acquisition of Technology which greatly expands on all tiers of technologies in ACOT, and Secrets of the Shroud which adds Theta. Since navigating ACOT's complex requirements for progressing, this website will go over that and some strategies to aid in your journey.

Delta

Delta is the first tier of unlockable technology in Ancient Cache of Technologies and is an Enigmatic Technology based on Dark Matter and Dark Energy. It is used by the fallen empires that scatter the galaxy.

Alpha

Alpha is an expansion to Delta. It is also an Enigmatic Technology based on Dark Matter and Dark Energy and was used by the ancient precursors that used to rule the galaxy.

Sigma

Sigma is the third tier of technology and is a Stellar Technology based on Stellarite, a highly volatile energy source nicknamed "Power Beyond Stars".

Phi

Phi is the fourth tier of technology, the only tier of Phanon Technologies and used by Phanon Corps, an intergalactic paramilitary service.

Theta

Theta is the fifth tier of technology, it is based around a shroud master. An extremely powerful being from the shroud. The technology is very potent, only bested by Omega.

Omega

Omega is the sixth and final tier of the technologies. It is an Omegan Technology created by the founders of the Omegan Metaverse, by far the most powerful Metaverse.
