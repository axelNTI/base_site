Gigastructural Engineering & More

Gigastructural Engineering is the most fleshed-out mod for Stellaris. It is primarily a mod for new megastructures and many of which dwarf the other ones, but it also adds more crises and events. It offers advanced customizability allowing you to enable and disable specific features. Therefore, you can shape the game to your liking with perhaps only a few additional features or everything.

Ancient Cache of Technologies

Do you want a complete power trip? Then Ancient Cache of Technologies is the mod for you. It adds new tiers of technology far beyond what is achievable in the base game, but beware, it is not for free. To unlock each one, you must overcome varying but challenging hurdles. It has built-in support for Gigastructural Engineering, allowing you to combine their powers to become absurdly powerful.

NSC2

The base game is quite limited in planning when it comes to combat. Try to get the biggest ships with the biggest guns. NSC2 or New Ship Classes 2 changes this. There is now a multitude of new ship types that add specific roles, each of which is crucial in gaining tactical supremacy over your enemy.

UI Overhaul Dynamic

The UI in Stellaris is imperfect, but adding UI Overhaul Dynamic is a step in the right direction. It scales the UI according to your resolution and sub-mods such as UI Overhaul Dynamic - Extended Topbar shows all resources on your resource bar, instead of forcing you to access a separate drop-down menu.

Link to Steampage

Tiny Outliner v2

Do you want to improve your UI even more? Then add Tiny Outliner v2. It declutters your outliner, making each div take up less space, and allowing you to see more stuff without any loss of information.

Link to Steampage

The Merger of Rules

The Merger of Rules is an essential mod for anyone who plays with multiple different gameplay-altering mods. It will improve your stability while reducing crashes, errors, and desyncs. One important aspect regarding the mod is that it needs to be at the absolute end of your playset. It will not work properly if any mod is below it. Link to Steampage
